@startuml
title NGO Donation Management Process

|Donor|
start
:Make Donation; 
:Enter Donation Details; 

|System|
:Validate Payment/Details; 
if (Valid?) then (No) 
    :Show Error Message; 
    stop
else (Yes) 
    :Save Donation Record; 
    :Notify Branch Manager; 
endif

|Branch Manager|
:View Received Donations; 
:Select Pending Donation; 
:View Available Volunteers; 
:Assign Donation to Volunteer; 

|System|
:Update Donation Status to "Assigned"; 
:Notify Volunteer; 

|Volunteer|
:View Assigned Tasks; 
:Perform Pickup / Task; 
:Mark Donation as "Completed"; 

|System|
:Update Status to "Completed"; 
:Log Completion History; 

|Branch Manager|
:View Donation Status (Completed); 

|System|
:Generate Receipt (Optional); 

|Donor|
:Receive Receipt; 
stop
@enduml
